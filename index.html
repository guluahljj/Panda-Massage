Frequently Asked Questions

How early should I arrive?
We recommend arriving 10-15 minutes prior to your appointment time. This allows you time to fill out any necessary intake forms, use the restroom, and begin to relax before your session starts.
Can I get a massage if I have an injury?
In many cases, yes. Massage can help recovery. However, please inform us of any injuries or medical conditions when booking and tell your therapist before the session starts so they can adjust the pressure and avoid sensitive areas.
What is your cancellation policy?
We understand things happen. Please notify us at least 24 hours in advance if you need to cancel or reschedule.
Is there parking available?
Yes, we have ample parking space right in front of our building at 1000 W Wisconsin Ave. It is free and convenient.
